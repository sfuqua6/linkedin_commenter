Setup
1

Unzip and load

Open chrome://extensions → enable Developer Mode → Load Unpacked → select the folder.

2

Connect a provider

Click the diamond icon in your toolbar. Pick Anthropic, OpenAI, Ollama, or any custom endpoint. Enter your key if needed.

3

Use it

Go to linkedin.com. Every post now has a Draft button. Click it, pick a tone, generate, paste.

Tones
Thoughtful
Adds nuance. Asks a smart follow-up. Sounds like someone who sat with the idea.
Contrarian
Respectful pushback. Offers what the post might be missing.
Storyteller
Brief personal anecdote that connects. Two sentences of story, one of takeaway.
Tactical
Concrete tip or framework. Specific enough to act on today.
